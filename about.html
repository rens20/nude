ABOUT US

Coworking is a work style where different individuals or teams share a working environment. This web application aims to connect individuals or teams looking for shared workspaces with property owners offering such spaces. It allows users to list properties, search for workspaces, and manage their listings. The application provides features for both property owners and coworkers to facilitate the process of finding and renting shared workspaces.
